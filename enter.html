Save Water

Don't Waste Water ,Save for Future Generations

Keep the tap close while doing hand wash, tooth brush, face wash, washing dishes, etc. Collect rain water during rainy season to use in toilet flush, watering plants, sprinkle in garden, etc. Using raw water like sea water or non-purified water in toilet is also good.

  • Never waste even a drop of water
  • No water, no life
  • Hold your grip and stop that drip
